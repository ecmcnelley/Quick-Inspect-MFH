## üìã NSPIRE INSPECTION AREAS

### Critical Safety Requirements

#### **Life Safety**

1. **Smoke Alarms (REQUIRED)**
   - Every bedroom
   - Hallways serving bedrooms
   - Each floor level
   - Type: Photoelectric or ionization, UL listed
   - Status: Working, battery-operated or hardwired
   - **Violation Level: 3 (Life-Threatening)**

2. **Carbon Monoxide Alarms (REQUIRED)**
   - Within 15 feet of bedrooms if fuel-burning appliances present
   - Near fuel-burning appliances
   - Type: Electrochemical sensor
   - **Violation Level: 3 (Life-Threatening)**

3. **GFI Protection (REQUIRED)**
   - Kitchen: All outlets within 6 feet of sink
   - Bathrooms: All bathroom outlets
   - Outdoor receptacles
   - Garage outlets
   - **Testing: Push test button monthly**
   - **Violation Level: 2 (Serious)**

4. **Railings (REQUIRED)**
   - Stairways: 3 or more risers require railings
   - Height: 34-38 inches from stair nosing
   - Graspable: 1.25 to 2 inches diameter
   - Secure: No movement when tested
   - **Violation Level: 2-3 (depending on height/exposure)**

#### **Structural Integrity**

1. **Walls and Ceilings**
   - No holes, cracks, or missing sections
   - Proper attachment
   - No water damage or stains
   - **Defective Paint: Special concern for pre-1978 housing**

2. **Flooring**
   - No tripping hazards
   - Proper installation
   - No water damage
   - Adequate for intended use

3. **Doors**
   - Entry doors: Minimum 32" clear opening (36" door)
   - Height: Minimum 80 inches
   - Functioning locks
   - Deadbolts on entry doors
   - Self-closing on fire-rated doors

4. **Windows**
   - All operable
   - Locks functioning
   - No broken glass
   - Weatherstripping intact
   - Screens present where applicable

#### **Electrical Systems**

1. **Outlets and Switches**
   - Covers on all outlets and switches
   - No exposed wiring
   - Proper spacing (no point >6 feet from outlet)
   - Securely fastened
   - Functioning correctly

2. **Lighting**
   - Adequate lighting in all rooms
   - Functioning fixtures
   - No exposed wiring
   - Proper bulb types

#### **Plumbing Systems**

1. **Water Supply**
   - Hot and cold water available
   - Adequate pressure
   - Temperature: Maximum 120¬∞F at fixtures
   - No leaks

2. **Drainage**
   - All drains functional
   - No slow drainage
   - No sewage odors
   - Proper venting

3. **Fixtures**
   - Toilets: Secure, flush properly, no leaks
   - Sinks: Secure, drains work, no leaks
   - Tubs/Showers: Secure, drains work, no leaks
   - Proper caulking and sealing

#### **Heating and Cooling**

1. **Heating System (REQUIRED)**
   - Adequate heat in all rooms
   - Functioning controls
   - Proper venting (fuel-burning systems)
   - No gas leaks
   - Annual inspection/maintenance

2. **Cooling (if provided)**
   - Adequate cooling
   - Functioning controls
   - Proper installation

3. **Ventilation (REQUIRED)**
   - Bathrooms: Window or exhaust fan
   - Kitchens: Range hood or window
   - Adequate air exchange

#### **Appliances**

1. **Refrigerator**
   - Maintaining proper temperature (35-38¬∞F)
   - Seals intact
   - Shelves/drawers functioning
   - No excessive noise
   - Clean and sanitary

2. **Stove/Range**
   - All burners functioning
   - Oven working and accurate temperature
   - No gas leaks
   - Knobs/controls functioning
   - Clean and maintained

3. **Range Hood**
   - Fan functioning
   - Light working
   - Filter present and clean
   - Exhausts outside (preferred)

4. **Water Heater (REQUIRED CHECKS)**
   - Temperature/Pressure Relief (TPR) valve present
   - TPR valve functioning
   - Drain pan (if in living space)
   - Proper venting (gas/oil)
   - Maximum 120¬∞F at fixtures
   - No rust or corrosion
   - No leaks

5. **Other Appliances**
   - Functioning as intended
   - Proper installation
   - No safety hazards

## üîç INSPECTION SEVERITY LEVELS

### NSPIRE Three-Tier System

#### **Level 3 - Life-Threatening**
Deficiencies that pose immediate threat to life, health, or safety:
- Missing/non-functioning smoke alarms
- Missing/non-functioning CO2 alarms
- Gas leaks
- Exposed electrical wiring
- Structural failure
- Missing railings (4+ risers, significant height)
- No heat in winter
- Sewage backup
- No water supply

**Action Required:** Immediate correction (24-48 hours)

#### **Level 2 - Serious**
Deficiencies that could lead to Level 3 if not corrected:
- GFI outlets not functioning
- Water heater without TPR valve
- Broken windows
- Inoperable locks on entry doors
- Significant water leaks
- Non-functioning heating/cooling (per lease)
- Plumbing problems
- Electrical problems (not immediately dangerous)

**Action Required:** 30 days maximum

#### **Level 1 - Minor**
Deficiencies that do not pose immediate threat but affect livability:
- Cosmetic damage
- Minor paint issues (not defective paint)
- Cabinet doors not closing properly
- Minor caulking issues
- Appliance performance issues (not safety)

**Action Required:** Reasonable timeframe (often next turnover)

## üìä PROGRAM-SPECIFIC REQUIREMENTS

### HOME Program Compliance

#### Property Standards (24 CFR 92.251)
- Must meet HUD's Housing Quality Standards (HQS)
- Local code requirements
- Lead-based paint requirements (pre-1978)
- Accessibility requirements (Section 504)
- Uniform accessibility standards

#### Inspection Requirements
- Initial inspection before occupancy
- Annual inspections for rental housing
- Special inspections as needed
- Compliance period: 5-20 years depending on assistance

#### Documentation Required
- Written inspection report
- Photos of deficiencies
- Correction timeline
- Evidence of correction
- Tenant notification

### LIHTC Program Compliance

#### Physical Inspection Requirements (IRC Section 42)
- Minimum of 20% of units inspected annually
- Random selection process
- Three-year cycle (all units inspected within 3 years)
- Common areas inspection
- Building systems inspection

#### Habitability Standards
- Must meet local housing codes
- Must meet HQS standards minimum
- Life-threatening deficiencies must be corrected immediately
- Other deficiencies within reasonable time

#### State Agency Requirements
- Varies by state
- May exceed federal minimums
- UPCS/NSPIRE/HQS standards
- Additional inspections may be required

### USDA Rural Development Compliance

#### Section 515 Requirements (7 CFR 3560.103)
- Annual property inspections
- Pre-occupancy inspections
- Special compliance reviews
- Common area inspections
- Site and grounds inspection

#### Management and Occupancy Review (MOR)
- Physical property inspection
- Tenant file review
- Financial records review
- Rent comparability analysis

#### Section 538 Requirements
- Annual inspections by lender
- HUD/FHA standards compliance
- Third-party professional inspectors
- HQS or similar standard

## üö® CRITICAL VIOLATIONS THAT MUST BE FLAGGED

### Immediate Action Required (Level 3)

1. **No functioning smoke alarm in bedroom**
   - Citation: NSPIRE Section 5.1
   - Penalty: Possible funding loss, liability
   - Action: Install within 24 hours

2. **No CO2 alarm near fuel-burning appliances**
   - Citation: NSPIRE Section 5.1
   - Penalty: Possible funding loss, liability  
   - Action: Install within 24 hours

3. **Gas leak detected**
   - Citation: NSPIRE Section 4.2
   - Penalty: Possible funding loss, severe liability
   - Action: Evacuate, repair immediately

4. **No heat in winter**
   - Citation: Warranty of habitability
   - Penalty: Rent abatement, possible funding loss
   - Action: Restore within 24 hours

5. **Missing railings (4+ risers)**
   - Citation: NSPIRE Section 3.4
   - Penalty: Possible funding loss, liability
   - Action: Install within 30 days

### Serious Deficiencies (Level 2)

1. **GFI outlets not functioning in kitchen/bathroom**
   - Citation: NSPIRE Section 4.1
   - Action: Repair within 30 days

2. **Water heater without TPR valve**
   - Citation: NSPIRE Section 4.3
   - Action: Install within 30 days

3. **Entry door lock not functioning**
   - Citation: Security requirements
   - Action: Repair within 48 hours

4. **Broken window**
   - Citation: Weatherization/security
   - Action: Repair within 30 days

### Minor Deficiencies (Level 1)

1. **Cosmetic paint issues (not defective paint)**
   - Action: Next turnover or within 60 days

2. **Cabinet door not closing properly**
   - Action: Next turnover or reasonable time

3. **Minor caulking issues**
   - Action: Next maintenance cycle
